about

Hello! I am a MSc Machine Learning student at University College London, I am interested in understanding intelligence through the scope of both machine learning theory and theoretical neuroscience. My research focuses on developing novel machine learning algorithms, drawing inspiration from biological systems, particularly how neural populations process information about uncertainty and make actions. I work with various ML techniques including graphical models, reinforcement learning, and Bayesian statistics, hoping to create machine intelligence that emulate the adaptability of natural intelligence. Before this, I worked as a photographer and cinematographer while spending a brief time at the Beijing Film Academy.
